My Favorite NBA Players: Points Over Time

A look at season point totals for some of the greatest scorers in NBA history — from Pete Maravich's explosive early career to LeBron James' remarkable longevity. Each season total reflects the raw points accumulated across all regular season games played that year.

Season Point Totals by Player

Each line traces a single player's cumulative point total across their career, increasing with every season played. Steeper slopes reveal dominant scoring years, while flatter stretches reflect reduced playing time or injury-shortened seasons. Hover over a line to highlight a player.

Relative Scoring Share Over Time

The streamgraph below shows each player's point totals as flowing bands stacked over time. The width of each band reflects how many points a player scored in a given season relative to the rest of the group — making it easy to see who was dominating during any era.

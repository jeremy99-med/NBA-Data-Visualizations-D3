My Favorite NBA Players: Points Over Time

A look at season point totals for some of the greatest scorers in NBA history — from Pete Maravich's explosive early career to LeBron James' remarkable longevity. Each season total reflects the raw points accumulated across all regular season games played that year.

Season Totals by Player

Each line traces a single player's cumulative selected stat total across their career, increasing with every season played. Steeper slopes reveal years dominant in that stat, while flatter stretches reflect reduced playing time or injury-shortened seasons. Hover over a line to highlight a player.

Relative Season Total Share Over Time

The streamgraph below shows each player's stat totals as flowing bands stacked over time. The width of each band reflects how many points/assists/rebounds/3pt/steal/blocks/games played a player has made in a given season relative to the rest of the group — making it easy to see who was dominating during any era.

Data source

Season totals sourced from Basketball Reference. Data covers all regular seasons for each player through retirement or the most recent completed season.
